1. Executive Summary: A Strategic Proposal.

Bamboo Zimbabwe Trust respectfully proposes to the International Centre for Bamboo and Rattan (ICBR) and the International Bamboo and Rattan Organisation (INBAR) to be appointed as the Bamboo Ambassador for Zimbabwe (pending appointment).

"Proving the commercial viability of a sustainable future."

business_center

Role & Coordination Mandate

We seek to serve as the recognized national focal partner to coordinate bamboo development, translate global knowledge into local impact, and drive investments and innovations that deliver tangible benefits for Zimbabwe's economy, environment and communities.

Zimbabwe possesses immense yet underutilised bamboo potential. Through this partnership, we will unlock that potential responsibly, scientifically and inclusively.
