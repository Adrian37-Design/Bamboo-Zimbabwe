ENVIRONMENTAL SCAN

2. Rationale for Appointment (Part 1)

Zimbabwe has enormous bamboo potential with indigenous and naturalised species. Despite this, the sector remains unstructured and underdeveloped, with no dedicated national bamboo policy or coordinating body.

bar_chart Strategic Objectives of Appointment

CENTRAL LIAISON

Proposed Appointment Goals:

  • Central Authority: Establish a central authority to guide and coordinate national bamboo initiatives.
  • Primary Liaison: Serve as the primary liaison between government, investors, international partners, and local communities.
  • Curated Pilots: Lead demonstration projects proving bamboo's economic, environmental, and climate impacts.
  • Investment Mobilization: Attract domestic and international capital for sector-wide scale-up and enterprise growth.
